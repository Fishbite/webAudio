How Do I Use This App?

turn your volume down and plug in your earphones

select a voice from the drop-down list play a tune using the keyboard keys Q through P

When you've put a little tune together, it's time to record it!

Start Recording

press ALT + Z

you will hear a single beat of the kick drum and the "Ready To Record" button will show "Stop Recording"

Play That Tune

play the little tune that you want to record

Stop Recording

click the "Stop Recording" button when done

Test Your Recording

hit the play button on the media recorder to listen to your recording

Play Continuously

right click the media recorder and select "Loop" from the to repeat continuously

Save Your Recording

if you are happy! Right click the media player and save the music file to somewhere on your computer. You can at this point load the file into music editing software, such as Audacity to trim the start and end points

Play Your Saved Recording

click the "Browse" button to select the file you have just saved to disc

play the selected file by clicking key A

the music will loop continuously

Try Out The Drums Etc.

try out the drums, keys Z, X, C, V and M. Key K plays a hi-hat

Stop Playing Your Recording

click key D to stop playing the selected file

Record More Stuff

now! If you are all happy! You can start recording additional tunes and stuff on top of the recording you just made

when you are ready, click ALT + Z to start recording again

click key A to start playing the recording back and play some drum beats, or additional music

Play Back The Composition

go back to Test Your Recording above and don't forget to save it to your computer
